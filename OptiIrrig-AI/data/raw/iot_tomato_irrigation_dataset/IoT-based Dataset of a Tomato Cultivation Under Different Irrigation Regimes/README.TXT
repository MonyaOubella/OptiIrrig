The dataset is composed by 4 CSV files: 

“stuard_environmental_data.csv”: contains the data generated by a Milesight EM500 CO2 device, namely, timestamp (ms), device identifier, air moisture (%RH), air temperature (◦C), CO2 level (ppm), barometric pressure (hPa), and battery (%). The geographic coordinates of the device are (lat: 44.809362, lon:10.273168). The data are sampled every 10 minutes. 

“stuard_water_meter_data.csv”: contains the data generated by three Talkpool OY1310 Water Meter devices, one per tomato line. This CSV file contains timestamp (ms), device identifier, line (number), and water volume (m3). The geographic coordinates of these devices are (lat: 44.808673, lon: 10.272948) for Line #1, (lat: 44.808669, lon: 10.272988) for Line #2, (lat: 44.808667, lon: 10.273026) for Line #3. The data are generally sampled every 10 minutes. 

“soil_values.csv”: contains the data generated by 3 Milesight EM500 SMTC devices, one for each line. The CSV file contains timestamp (ms), line (number), device identifier, soil electrical conductivity (μS/cm), soil moisture (%RH), soil temperature (◦C). The geographic coordinates of the devices are (lat: 44.808729, lon: 10.272965) for Line #1, (lat: 44.808726, lon:10.273005) for Line #2, and (lat:44.808723, lon:10.273045) for Line #3. Data are sampled every 10 minutes. The soil sensors are positioned at a 20 cm depth. 

“indicators.csv”: contains the daily values of the following agronomic indicators: the Growing Degree Days (GDD) and the Heat Units (using 6 different methods). The Heat Units are calculated through the Agriware platform for the experimental crop, in the monitoring period. This CSV file contains midnight timestamp (ms), GDD value, Standard Day Degree (°C), daily mean temperature (°C), daily maximum temperature above the base temperature (°C), daily maximum temperature (°C), daily maximum temperature above the base temperature with reduction of the cutoff temperature (°C), Ontario unit (°C). The data are sampled once per day, with the base temperature (T_base) set at 10°C and the cutoff temperature (T_cutoff) set at 32°C.
